About

I am a PhD student studying mathematics at the University of Toronto, supervised by Yael Karshon. My current research deals with singular foliations, Lie groupoids, and diffeology. Starting in September 2023, I will be a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute in Bonn. I am also interested in convex geometry, in which I completed a master's project with Dmitry Faifman.

Here is my CV.
